DST-ABIGAIL-ENHANCED

Enhances Wendy's Abigail with combat, utility, and survivability upgrades.

中文

当前功能：
- 阿比盖尔锁定攻击目标时会立刻先劈一道雷。
- 只要目标还活着且仍然是阿比盖尔当前目标，就会每 2 秒继续电一次。
- 阿比盖尔每次命中目标都会刷新一次 60 秒、50% 降移速效果。
- 阿比盖尔永久获得 50% 移速提升。
- 阿比盖尔发光范围提升到原版的 7 倍。
- 每个阿比盖尔都会每 60 秒为附近玩家回复 9 点饱食度、9 点理智、9 点生命值。
- 阿比盖尔永久免疫雷击和火焰伤害。
- 阿比盖尔对地面上的耐久类物品使用“作祟”命令时，会尽量回满耐久和保质期。

耐久恢复覆盖：
- `finiteuses` 使用次数类物品
- `armor` 护甲耐久类物品
- `fueled` 燃料耐久类物品
- `perishable` 带保质期的物品

补充说明：
- 附近玩家回复效果按每个阿比盖尔单独结算，多只阿比盖尔聚在一起会更快回复。
- 当前版本没有做外部配置文件开关。

English

Current features:
- Abigail calls down lightning on her current attack target immediately.
- While that target stays alive and remains engaged, lightning strikes again every 2 seconds.
- Whenever Abigail hits a target, she refreshes a 60 second 50% movement slow.
- Abigail permanently gains 50% movement speed.
- Abigail's light radius is increased to 7x the vanilla radius.
- Every Abigail restores 9 hunger, 9 sanity, and 9 health to nearby players every 60 seconds.
- Abigail is permanently immune to lightning and fire damage.
- When Abigail uses her haunt command on a dropped durability-based item, it restores durability and freshness where supported.

Durability restoration covers:
- `finiteuses` use-count items
- `armor` condition-based armor items
- `fueled` fueled durability items
- `perishable` perishable items

Notes:
- Nearby player restoration is applied per Abigail, so grouped Abigails restore players faster.
- There are no external configuration options in this version.
